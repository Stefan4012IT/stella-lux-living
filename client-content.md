# Stella Lux — Client Content Direction

## Project overview

Stella Lux is a premium kindergarten / preschool brand located in Lux 51, Bežanijska kosa. The website should present Stella Lux as more than a standard kindergarten: a premium early childhood experience where care, space, learning, creativity, nature and high standards come together.

The client material should be used as a strategic direction, not copied literally. There are many repetitions, so the website content should be refined, structured and reduced into a clearer premium story.

## Main positioning

Stella Lux is a premium kindergarten created for parents who want more than childcare and more than an average preschool experience.

Core idea:

Stella Lux. Detinjstvo koje sija.
Where little stars begin to shine.

Supporting positioning:

Viši standard za najvažnije prve godine.
Premium Early Childhood Experience for a Bright Future.

## Brand meaning

Stella means star: every child has their own individuality, potential and light.

Lux means light, but also a higher standard: more care, more quality, more thoughtfulness, more space for growth.

The brand should feel warm, premium, bright, calm, soft and trustworthy. Avoid sounding cold, elitist or snobbish.

- Premium vrtić / preschool
- Location: Lux 51, Bežanijska kosa
- More than 500 m² of carefully designed space

## Visual direction

The visual style should be:
- Minimal
- Bright
- Soft premium
- Elegant but child-friendly
- Spacious layouts
- Large photography
- Subtle motion
- Rounded shapes
- Soft shadows
- Calm typography

Primary colors:
- Light blue: #adcbe0
- Warm beige
- White / off-white
- Optional deeper blue or muted gold only as accent

Use the logo on a light or transparent background. Since the uploaded logo is white, prepare layout variations where it can sit on blue, beige or image overlay backgrounds.

### Experience pillars

Use 6 premium pillars instead of a long list:

1. Love & Care
Emotional safety, individual support and a relationship where every child is truly seen.

2. Reggio-Inspired Learning
Learning through space, art, light, materials, questions and exploration.

3. Learning by Doing
Practical experiences, projects and activities where children understand the world through real situations.

4. Holistic Development
English, culture, art, science and nature as part of everyday growth.

5. Culture & Nature Experiences
Outdoor time, walks, museums, theatres, galleries and cultural experiences.

6. High Standards from the Start
Accredited program, dedicated team and a carefully designed concept for the most important years of development.

## Tone of voice

Warm, premium, calm, confident, emotionally intelligent.

Avoid:
- Too much repetition
- Overusing the word luxury
- Sounding elitist
- Generic kindergarten phrases
- Overpromising educational results

Prefer:
- thoughtful
- carefully designed
- individual approach
- early years
- emotional safety
- space to grow
- standard of care
- childhood that shines
